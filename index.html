Welcome to My Personal Growth Story

Hello, my name is Hunter Pearce. I'm a 20-year-old college student passionate about weightlifting and volleyball.

Not long ago, I weighed around 360 pounds at 6'2". I was tired most of the time, unhappy with how I felt, and stuck in unhealthy routines. Eventually, I realized I needed to change — not for anyone else, but for myself.

This website shares my journey of weight loss, strength training, volleyball, mindset growth, and becoming a healthier version of myself both mentally and physically.

Thanks for being here. Explore the pages above to see how far I've come — and how far I plan to go.
